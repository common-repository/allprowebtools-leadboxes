Leadbox widget to integrate with the AllProWebTools cloud based CRM.

== Description ==

Add your AllProWebTools CRM lead boxes (signup forms) to your WordPress website.

AllProWebTools is an all-in-one toolkit for small businesses that includes a CRM, email marketing, and lead generation system.

Use this widget to display a lead box on your website to gather contact information from your visitors: setup autoresponders, email drip campaigns, surveys and more.

Take a test spin of our WordPress plugin - We would love to hear your feedback!

= Features =

* Lead box widget can be used in the sidebar, content, footer or where ever you like.
* Customizable "Thank You" page
* Customizable "Thank You" message
* Configure auto response to immediately send an email after submitting Lead box
* Trigger email campaigns to send on a set schedule
* Unlimited tagging of leads for later follow up
* Unlimted customizable fields allow you to get more information such as: age, height, favorite color, etc.
* Builtin security features include spam detection and firewall to reduce unwanted messages from spambots, spammers, and hacking tools such as fraudgpt.

= Serviceware =
This is a Serviceware plugin for WordPress which interfaces with the AllProWebTools cloud servers to process transactions.  Storing this information in the cloud allows all of your devices and websites to access this information and increase exposure of your products and services.  An active AllProWebTools account is required to use this plugin. A link will be provided to create a new account from within the plugin settings menu.

== Installation ==

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=HoZC38suBIc]

**Automatic Installation**

1. Login to your wordpress with wp-admin
1. Choose **Plugins** from the menu and click **Add New**
1. Search for **AllProWebTools Leadboxes** and then click **Install Now**
1. Once the plugin is installed, click **Activate Now**
1. Next see **Configuration** below.

**Manual Installation**

1. Unzip `allprowebtools-leadboxes.zip` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

**Demo Mode**

When you first install the plugin, it will be configured for Demo Mode.  Demo Mode comes pre-loaded with some sample lead boxes for you to play around with.

When you are ready to get started with your own account, simply follow the configuration instructions below:

**Configuration**

1. Activate the plugin.
1. Choose **AllProWebTools** from the menu in wp-admin
1. Click on the link that says **sign up for one here** to create an AllProWebTools account or if you already have an AllProWebTools account login to your AllProWebTools account.
1. From inside your AllProWebTools account, choose "settings" then "Administrator"
1. Enter the API key and API Auth you see into your WordPress plugin settings.
1. Click **Update Settings**
1. Now you can go to **Appearance** and **Widgets** to access your new AllProWebTools Leadbox Widget.
